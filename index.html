About

Hi, I'm Pratyush Ranjan Mishra — a software engineer who builds reliable backend systems and developer tooling for enterprise integration.

Much of my work sits where messy real-world protocols meet clean, usable tools: universal protocol workbenches like NexusLink and Conduit that put REST, Kafka, gRPC, SQL, WebSocket and MCP/LLM connections behind one console, plus libraries and transformers for financial message formats such as SWIFT and ISO. I work mainly in Java and Go, and I care about Enterprise Integration Patterns, clean APIs, and tools that make hard integrations feel simple.
